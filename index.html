Indian Engineering Company has experience of more than 40 years in electrical service industry. Located at the outskirts of Vadodara, Gujarat. The Company has well trained & dedicated Engineers and a highly skilled workforce for giving quality service to the industry.

Indian Engineering Company is one of the largest repair shop in field of Rewinding / Repairing of high voltage Motors/ Generators.

    Imagine a repair shop as under.

  • Land of 300000 sq.feet
  • Shop floor area with crane of 75000 sq.ft.
    • x
  • Cranes from 10, 20, 50, 100 and 300 Tons capacity.
  • Load trial upto 2 MW on full load.
  • No Load trial upto 20 MW on rated voltage and speed.
  • Captive power of 5 MW.
  • Repairing of Motor/ Generator upto 250 MW.

The company has been carrying out, on an average, 400 HT motor rewinding jobs, refurbishing and overhauling jobs per annum. The repair work and testing being carried out is strictly as per IS Standards. Material used for repairs is procured as per the relevant Specifications from reputed manufacturers and tested, if needed, at reputed Labs only.
